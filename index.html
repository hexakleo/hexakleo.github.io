About Me

Hexakleo, known as HK on GitHub, is a developer with a strong interest in programming and cybersecurity. Throughout his career, he has contributed to various open-source projects, demonstrating his commitment to the tech community.

One of his most notable projects is "skuld," an advanced tool written in Go that functions as a next-generation "stealer." This tool is designed to extract information from applications such as Discord, Chromium-based and Firefox-based browsers, cryptocurrency wallets, and more, from every user on every disk. It's important to note that this project is a proof of concept and is provided solely for educational purposes.

In addition to "skuld," Hexakleo has worked on other projects that reflect his interest in security and programming. His GitHub profile showcases a variety of repositories ranging from development tools to contributions to other open-source initiatives.

Through his activity on GitHub, Hexakleo has built a community of followers and collaborators who value his expertise and dedication to the world of software and cybersecurity. His focus on educational and open-source projects highlights his commitment to continuous learning and improving the tech community at large.
